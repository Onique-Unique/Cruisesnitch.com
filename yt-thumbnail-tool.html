How to setup the Youtube Thumbnail Compare & Review Tool:

  • Step 1: First, copy the bookmarklet code by clicking the "Copy Bookmarklet" button above.
  • Step 2: Create a new bookmark in your browser.
  • Step 3: Click more! and paste the copied bookmarklet code into the URL field.
  • Step 4: Save the bookmark.
  • Step 5: Go to a YouTube video search page and click the bookmark to activate the tool.
  • Step 6: If on mobile type the name of the tool in the address bar while on Youtube video search page and select.

Using The Tool In Your Browser!

Using The Youtube Thumbnail Compare & Review Tool:

  • Step 1: Open Youtube in your browser.
  • Step 2: Search for a video topic on Youtube.
  • Step 3: Scroll to see all the top ranking videos for your search/ topic of interest
  • Step 4: Click the address bar in your browser and type the name of your bookmark - (YT Thumbnail Comparison Tool)
  • Step 5: Select the bookmark while still on the Youtube search page.
  • Step 6: The Tool should take effect if instructions are followed properly. Enjoy!
