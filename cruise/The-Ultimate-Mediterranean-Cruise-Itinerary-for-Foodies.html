What are the best wine and cocktail pairings for Mediterranean cuisine?

Wine and cocktail pairings with Mediterranean cuisine can be the perfect combination.

Pairing the right wine with a Mediterranean meal can add depth and sweetness to the dish, while the right cocktail can bring out unique flavors.

Here are some of the best wine and cocktail pairings for Mediterranean cuisine:

  • Pinot Grigio: Crisp and light, this white wine is a perfect accompaniment to Mediterranean cuisine, especially seafood dishes like grilled fish and shrimp.
  • Riesling: A slightly sweet German white wine, this is an excellent choice for Mediterranean salads and light pasta dishes.
  • Sangiovese: An Italian red wine, this full-bodied selection is ideal for hearty Mediterranean dishes like beef, lamb, pork, and tomato-based sauces.
  • Aperol Spritz: This sparkling cocktail combines orange and herbal flavor, perfect for contemporary Mediterranean dishes like bruschetta.
  • Classic Martini: Balance out salty feta and olive tapenade with the boozy flavor of a classic martini.
  • Spiked Lemonade: A summer favorite, a spiked lemonade is perfect for any Mediterranean feast.
