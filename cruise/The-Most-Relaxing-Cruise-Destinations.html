How can you choose the best cabin for maximum relaxation on a cruise ship?

  • Figure out: what kind of relaxation best suits your needs. Do you want to be close to the pool? Away from traffic? An inside cabin to better weatherize?
  • Look for: cabins with upgraded amenities and options. Specialty mattresses, top-of-the-line linens, and comfortable pillows can help make a cruise cabin feel like a five-star hotel.
  • Consider: the size of the cabin. It should be large enough to accommodate your needs but not so small that you feel cramped.
  • If you are: considering a balcony cabin, compare the view options. A great view can make all the difference in maximizing relaxation.
  • Review: the ship's noise level ratings. Oftentimes, cabins at the back will be quieter than cabins at the front.
  • Look for: cabins that are close to deck amenities. If the spa, pool, or other amenities that make you feel relaxed are nearby, you can easily access them from your cabin.
  • Research: the cruise line's services and amenities. Look for things like stress-relieving treatments, yoga classes, and spa days.
  • Make sure: it has enough plugs for charging all of your devices. Nothing is worse than not having enough charging stations.
  • Look into: the cabin's soundproofing. Ask the cruise line how much soundproofing was used in the cabin's construction.
  • Take a: virtual tour of some of the cabins offered. Cruise lines often provide visual tours of the cabins, which can help you decide which one best suits your needs.
