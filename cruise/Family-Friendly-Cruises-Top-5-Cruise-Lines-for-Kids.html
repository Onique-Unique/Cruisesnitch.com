Family-Friendly Cruises: Top 5 Cruise Lines for Kids in 2023

When it comes to planning a family vacation, a cruise can be an excellent choice, offering something for everyone.

However, not all cruise lines are created equal when it comes to catering to kids. In 2023, several cruise lines stand out as top picks for family-friendly cruises. If you're looking for a memorable experience that both you and your children will love, here are the top 5 cruise lines for kids in 2023.

  • Disney Cruise Line: Nothing beats the Disney magic! Even during a family cruise, your family will have plenty of entertainment and unforgettable experiences. With nonstop activities and characters meet-and-greets, Disney Cruise Line is one of the best family cruise lines sailing in 2023.
  • Royal Caribbean: Royal Caribbean keeps their fleet of ships full of activities and entertainment for all ages. Enjoy mini-golf, water slides, sports courts, and several kid-friendly dining options for a wholesome cruise experience for the whole family.
  • Norwegian Cruise Line: If the family loves adventure, Norwegian Cruise Line is your go-to cruise line. From zip-lining and rock-climbing walls to indoor and outdoor play areas, families can keep their minds and bodies busy for hours.
  • Carnival Cruise Line: Surf simulators, poolside movies and several kid-friendly clubs make Carnival Cruise Line one of the top choices for family cruises. In 2023, Carnival offers a variety of cruises to top Caribbean cruise destinations.
  • Holland America Line: Holland America Line provides many cruise options that can allow families to explore exciting destinations such as Mexico, Alaska, and Canada. For a bit of fun, the line offers a Greenhouse Spa, art museum, and other kid-friendly activities.




What are some tips for keeping kids entertained during shore excursions on a family-friendly cruise?

  • Explore the local area: Most ports of call on a family-friendly cruise will offer a variety of interesting attractions for kids to explore and visit. From beaches and outdoor activities to cultural landmarks and museums, take the time to explore the area with your children and let their imaginations run wild.
  • Participate in shore excursions: Shore excursions often have activities that are tailored to families. From snorkeling and kayaking to city tours and scavenger hunts, there are plenty of opportunities to engage in exciting and fun activities with the whole family.
  • Take advantage of onboard activities: Most family-friendly cruises have a variety of onboard activities available. Make sure to check out the daily activity schedule and have your kids participate in activities like puzzle games, trivia contests, magic shows, and other kid-friendly activities.
  • Bring activities on the shore excursion: To make the most of your shore excursion, consider packing a bag of supplies to keep your kids entertained. Bring books, coloring books, portable toys, games, and snacks to help keep your kids occupied and happy during your shore excursion.

Bring a picnic blanket and some snacks and let your kids explore the beach, look for seashells, or build sandcastles. It is the perfect opportunity for the family to relax and catch up with each other.
