How can you prepare for and stay safe during high-intensity activities while at sea?

It's always wise to prepare for any activity, especially at sea where the elements can change quickly.

To stay safe while engaging in high-intensity activities at sea, it is important to adhere to the following tips:

  • Research the area you will be in: Make sure to check the tide schedules, weather forecasts, and nearby locations that may be in danger of rough waters.
  • Wear the right gear: Make sure to wear the right gears when required, such as life jacket, helmet, and other proper safety gear for your activity.
  • Stay aware: Pay attention to your surroundings and remain aware if you feel you are in a sketchy area.
  • Don't overexert yourself: If you feel tired or weak, stop and rest. Don't push yourself to the point of exhaustion.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your high-intensity activities at sea will be safe and enjoyable.
