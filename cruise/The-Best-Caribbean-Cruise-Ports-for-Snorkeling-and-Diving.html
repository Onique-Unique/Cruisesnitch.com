Are you ready to explore the Caribbean's most incredible snorkeling and diving spots in 2023?

Cruise to these 7+ best Caribbean Cruise Ports for an experience like no other!

  • Negril, Jamaica: Explore the spectacular coral reefs and vibrant marine life while you snorkel in the warm turquoise waters of Negril.
  • Labadee, Haiti: Take on the thrill of snorkeling in Haiti's crystal clear waters on the north coast and explore the amazing variety of sea creatures living down there.
  • Cozumel, Mexico: Defined as "the Mecca of snorkelers," Cozumel is renowned for its spectacular coral and diverse marine life that can be seen from the surface.
  • Montego Bay, Jamaica: Take in the beauty of the Caribbean Sea from the surface by snorkeling the shallow waters of this stunning port.
  • Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands: Dive deep and swim among beautiful coral gardens, sponges, and a wide array of brightly colored marine life.
  • St. Thomas, U.S. Virgin Islands: Indulge in the perfect snorkeling and diving experience as you swim among the vibrant coral gardens and eye-catching marine life.
  • Aruba: Spend time observing the abundant and colorful fish species while exploring the Aruban waters teeming with life.
  • Curacao: Let your dive mask be your portal to the vibrant coral reefs and marine life, including colorful starfish and sea turtles.
  • Nassau, Bahamas: Spot the brilliantly colored parrotfish, moray eels and sea fans that dot the waters of Nassau.
  • Roatan, Honduras: Take your pick from the wide array of dive and snorkel sites and explore the colorful, vibrant marine life dwelling beneath the surface.
