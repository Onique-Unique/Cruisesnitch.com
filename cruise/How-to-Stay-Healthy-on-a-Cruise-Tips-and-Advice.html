How to Stay Healthy on a Cruise in 2023: Tips and Advice

Cruising is a fantastic way to relax, unwind and explore new destinations. However, it's essential to prioritize your health and wellness during your trip to ensure you can fully enjoy your experience.

Whether you're a seasoned cruiser or a first-timer, learning how to stay healthy on a cruise in 2023 is critical. This guide will provide you with tips and advice to help you maintain good health and make the most of your journey.

What are the best ways to stay active and fit on a cruise ship?

  • Try the ship's gym: - Many cruise ships offer a variety of fitness equipment for you to use, including treadmills, stationary bikes, and weights. Check to see what your ship has available and take advantage of all the fitness options you have!
  • Walk around whenever you can: - The easiest way to stay active on a cruise ship is simply to walk wherever you go. Take the stairs instead of the elevator, walk around the deck or promenade, and take the long way to wherever you need to be.
  • Participate in on-board activities: - Most ships have a variety of activities available, including classes like Pilates, yoga, and even aerobics. Check the daily schedule when you board the ship to see what's offered.
  • Take a swim: - Swimming is a great way to get active while you're on a cruise. Most ships have swimming pools, hot tubs, and even water slides. Take a few laps, or just lounge in the pool and enjoy the sunshine.
  • Go for an adventurous excursion: - If the ship makes a port stop, take advantage of it and get off the boat for a bit. Explore the area by going for a hike, going snorkeling, or participating in any other active activity the destination has to offer.



How can you maintain a healthy diet while still enjoying the onboard dining options?

  • Start each day with a nutritious breakfast: Choose a meal with a variety of protein, whole grains and fruits/vegetables.
  • Make smart meal decisions: Opt for lean proteins such as grilled fish, chicken or turkey, choose whole grain options such as brown rice, quinoa and oatmeal, and bulk up on vegetables and fruits.
  • Watch your portion size: Ask for smaller portions and balance out your meal with plenty of vegetables to fill you up.
  • Give yourself treat options: Treat yourself to a special dessert or a snack after a day of sightseeing.
  • Drink plenty of water: This can help you stay hydrated and avoid foods that are high in sugar and calories.
  • Choose activities that promote healthy living: Take a yoga class, try out a spinning class or go for a walk on the deck.
