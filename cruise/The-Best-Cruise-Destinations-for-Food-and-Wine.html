How can you best plan and budget for food and wine experiences on a cruise?

  • Research ahead of time: Look online and get familiar with the different food and wine experiences the cruise is offering. Make sure the prices and inclusions are within your budget and plan accordingly.
  • Set a budget: Before you set out on your cruise, determine how much you are willing to spend on food and wine experiences.
  • Choose wisely: Decide on the experiences you would like to partake in ahead of time so that you can plan and budget accordingly.
  • Look for discounts: Check for available discounts and promotions on food and wine experiences to get the most bang for your buck.
  • Seek out free dining options: Some cruise ships have complimentary buffets and meals during certain times of day. Take advantage of those opportunities whenever possible to save money.
  • Choose card or cash?: Before boarding the ship, decide which payment method you are going to use. Some ships offer discounts to those who pay with cash or a certain type of credit card.
  • Bring your own food: Consider bringing your own snacks and drinks to help you save a little money on the food and wine experiences.
  • Get to know your dining options: Make sure you are familiar with all the restaurants, cafes, and bars on the ship so that you can make the most of all the exciting food and drinks available when you are onboard.



What are the unique dining or wine-tasting events offered on specific cruise lines or itineraries?

  • Private Winemakers Dinners on Royal Caribbean: Enjoy fine wine and delicious food prepared by experienced chefs while being guided through renowned winemakers and their special vintages
  • Foodie Walking Tour on Celebrity Cruises: Stop at local eateries, bakeries and wineries to sample classic and traditional recipes from the region.
  • Gourmet Cuisine on Disney Cruises: Taste the flavors of different parts of the world and get the inside scoop on signature dishes from the experts in the ship's kitchen.
  • Pub Crawls on Princess Cruises: Move from bar to bar, tasting different beverages and dishes, in an entertaining and informative night.
  • Wine Tasting Party on Norwegian Cruise Line: Sample classic wines from around the world and learn from on-board sommeliers about the different types and flavors.
  • World Cuisine Seminars on Carnival Cruises: Get an in-depth look into the classic specialties of different cultures with sampling of produce and dishes indigenous to the region.
  • Global Menu Dishes on Holland America Line: Experience fine cuisine with this voyage focused on traditional dishes and flavors from each destination.
