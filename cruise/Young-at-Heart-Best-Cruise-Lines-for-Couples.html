How can couples make the most of onboard amenities and dining options to enhance their experience?

  • Explore the Ship: Take advantage of the first day to check out what the ship has to offer like the pool, gym, spa, casinos, lounges, sports and entertainment venues, kids play areas, restaurants, and shops.
  • Visit the A La Carte Restaurants: Most cruises offer a wide variety of unique cuisine and dining experiences that you won't find at home. Make sure to sample the specialty items at least once or twice to really enjoy the culinary delights the ship has to offer.
  • Take Part in Onboard Activities: Whether you are participating in a class, lecture, or performance, take advantage of the daily activities that the ship has to offer. Make sure to participate in at least one activity each day.
  • Adopt the Room Sherpa: If you have time, make sure to ask what the entertainment staff has planned or get advice on how to enhance your vacation experience.
  • Embrace the Spirit of Exploration: Step outside of your comfort zone and explore the ports of call and the local destinations beyond. Get out and get lost, and you will find hidden treasures and serendipity around every corner.
  • Take Advantage of All-Inclusive Amenities: Many cruise ships offer all-inclusive packages that allow you to partake in all of the onboard activities, from the nightly shows, to the casino, and spa treatments.
  • Pamper Yourselves: Take advantage of any spa services that the ship offers, or grab a couples massage to really relax and unwind.
  • Utilize the Room Amenities: Make sure to use the amenities the cruise ship offers like the housekeeping staff, room service, and in-room movies to really take advantage of your time on the ship.



What should couples consider when selecting a cabin or suite for their cruise, and how can they maximize their comfort and privacy?

  • Budget: Decide how much youâ€™re willing to spend on the cabin or suite and research options within your budget.
  • Cabin size: Consider whether you need extra space to spread out and relax or if you're comfortable in a smaller space.
  • Amenities: Look for features that will enhance your cruise experience, such as a private balcony, spacious sitting area, or a mini-bar.
  • Location: Ensure that your chosen cabin is located near the areas of the ship that you plan to use the most.
  • Privacy: If you need more privacy, book a higher deck with more secluded cabins or consider a suite with separate areas to ensure privacy.
  • Noise: Ask about potential sound levels and proximity to areas such as the entertainment deck and pool deck to help you decide your ideal location.
  • Layout: Every ship and cabin can have a different layout, so make sure to look at the layout before you book to ensure you get a comfortable cabin without any unpleasant surprises.
  • Upgrades: Many ships offer upgrades, so consider if you'd like to take advantage of an upgrade package or special amenities.
