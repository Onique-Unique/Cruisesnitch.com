How to choose the best shore excursions to capture the perfect Instagram shot at each port?

To start, use online reviews and photos to get a feel for the destination. There are a few key points to consider when selecting an excursion that gives you the best vantage point for capturing the perfect photo:

  • Location: A great Instagram shot is all about the location. Look up attractions near the dock and research the best spot for photos.
  • Time of Day: It's best to select activities that provide optimal lighting for photography. That means scenes at sunrise and sunset are ideal for outdoor photography.
  • Environment: Natural attractions such as beaches, mountains, and waterfalls will provide a picturesque backdrop. Consider what your ideal subject will be in the photo and pick excursions accordingly.
  • Tour Group: Not all tour groups are created equal. Smaller groups will give you more flexibility in terms of photography and access to prime spots.

Taking these points into consideration will help you select the best shore excursions to capture your perfect Instagram shot at each port.
