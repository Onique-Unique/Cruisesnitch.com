The Best Cruise Destinations for Nature Lovers in 2023

For nature lovers, a cruise can be the perfect way to explore some of the world's most stunning natural wonders.

From glaciers and fjords to tropical rainforests and coral reefs, the best cruise destinations for nature lovers in 2023 offer a wealth of natural beauty and diversity. While the experience of cruising through nature can be awe-inspiring, it's essential to plan your journey carefully to get the most out of your trip.

  • Galapagos Islands, Ecuador: Immerse yourself in a land where nature still rules, with its tapestry of blue lagoons, stark volcanic islands and diverse wildlife species.
  • Antarctica: Entombed by ancient glaciers, explore this pristine continent and its myriad of icy inlets, rugged mountains and ice cliffs.
  • Norwegian Fjords: Cruise along rugged coastlines and past towering cliffs, carved by ancient glaciers as far as the eyes can see.
  • Costa Rica: Spot bright macaws and sloths as you journey on a boat through the idyllic canals of Manuel Antonio National Park.
  • Alaska: From bald eagles to killer whales, explore the ice cold waters of Alaska and the vast wildlife that inhabit this majestic landscape.
  • Patagonia: Here, towering snow-capped peaks enclose deep fjords and pristine glacial lakes.
  • Hawaii: Cruise along a string of beautiful Hawaiian islands, from verdant rain-forests to secluded black and white sandy beaches.
  • Crooked Island Passage: Travel along the Crooked Island Passage and discover its spectacular mix of desert, tropical savannahs and lush forests.
  • South Pacific: Take in the marine life of the South Pacific by cruising past remote atolls and soaring volcanoes.
  • Mediterranean Sea: Glide past the rocky coves and vibrant coastal towns of the Mediterranean Sea.
  • Red Sea: Sail through the magical Red Sea and explore its vibrant coral reefs while basking in the sun.
  • Caribbean: Cruise to crystal clear waters and powdery white sand beaches of the Caribbean islands.
  • Maldives: Immerse yourself in the serenity and beauty of the Maldives by cruising around the lush tropical islands and amazing lagoons.
  • Seychelles: Explore the luxurious seclusion of the Seychelles from the shimmering blue waters to the white sand beaches.
  • Amazon River: Take in the vast biodiversity of the Amazon River through its winding channels, thriving waterfalls and pristine rainforest.
  • Danube Delta: Cruise along the Danube Delta and witness the unique sculptures of nature that form it, from enchanting wetlands to endless sandbars.
  • Great Barrier Reef: Drift along the crystal-clear waters and explore the colorful coral gardens teeming with exotic marine life.

How can you make your cruise more environmentally friendly and sustainable?

  • Save water: - Only take short showers and turn off the faucet as often as possible.
  • Reduce your waste: - Pack light, bring reusable water bottles and food containers, and only purchase items that come in recyclable packaging.
  • Save energy: - Turn off lights, electrical appliances, and air conditioning when not in use.
  • Consider your transportation: - Use public transportation or rent bicycles to explore shoreside locations instead of taking the cruise ship to different ports.
  • Choose green-friendly ships: - Investigate which cruise lines have adopted environmental policies and initiatives designed to reduce their carbon footprint and decrease emissions.
  • Offer suggestions: - Contact the ship's crew and management if you have identified areas of improvement that could help make the cruise more environmentally friendly.
  • Buy local: - Support local businesses by purchasing local souvenirs and produce, as well as patronizing restaurants run by locals.
  • Share your message: - Talk to fellow passengers about the importance of sustainable cruising so that more people take part in protecting the environment.
