What are the top must-see attractions on the Darwin Sunset Cruise, and why are they worth visiting?

  • Darwin Harbour: This is the ideal place to spot local wildlife and enjoy the astonishing sunset view. With its many islands, towering cliffs and mangroves, there is plenty of scenery to explore.
  • Tiwi Islands: Explore these secluded and pristine tropical islands off the coast of Darwin. Here you can get an up close experience with sea turtles, manta rays, and some of the most vibrant coral reefs.
  • Cullen Bay: This is a picture-perfect waterfront destination with waterfalls, walking trails, a lovely beach and plenty of tucked away spots perfect for taking in the silence and watching the sunset.
  • East Point Reserve: Here you can take in stunning views of the iconic Darwin Harbour, take a leisurely walk around the nature reserve, or spot a rare animal or two.
  • Mindil Beach and Markets: An essential Darwin stop for sunset cruisers, Mindil Beach is one of the best locations in the city for gorgeous sunsets and evening strolls. There are also some of the most popular evening markets held here which offer plenty of tasty treats, local trinkets and unique souvenirs.
