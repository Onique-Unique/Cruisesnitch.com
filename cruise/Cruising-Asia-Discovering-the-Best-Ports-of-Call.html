What are the top cultural experiences to look out for in Asian ports of call?

Exploring the culture of Asian ports of call is an excellent way to make the most out of your vacation.

From colorful festivals to absorbing traditional performances, there are endless experiences to dive into and enjoy.

Here are some of the top cultural experiences to look out for as you travel around Asia:

  • Markets: Traditional street markets are incredibly popular in many Asian cities, and offer visitors the chance to admire and buy everything from snacks to handmade local crafts. Explore China's bustling Silk and Pearl Markets, Thailand's floating markets, or the vibrant night markets of Singapore.
  • Street Food: Street food is a great way to sample local delicacies, and Asian cities are full of vendors selling an array of dishes for all palates. From Japanese ramen to steaming Chinese dumplings, you can sample a real range of unique flavours.
  • Religious Sites: Asia is home to many sacred sites and ancient temples, from the Kiyomizu-dera Temple in Kyoto to Hanoi's Temple of Literature. These spiritual places are perfect for reflecting on ancient traditions and learning more about the local culture.
  • Festivals: Asian cities host a range of vibrant festivals throughout the year. Japan's Gion Matsuri is a spectacular sight, and in The Philippines, Santacruzan is a widely recognized faith-based event. There are endless cultural celebrations to explore, so make sure to research any events happening during your port of call.
  • Shows: Traditional Asian performances are an absolute must-see, from dramatic Chinese Peking opera to classic Indonesian wayang puppet shows. Ticket prices vary, but they are worth the cost for a unique cultural insight.

So whether you're sampling street food, exploring ancient temples, or enjoying a show, there are plenty of opportunities for cultural discovery when you're visiting Asian ports of call.
