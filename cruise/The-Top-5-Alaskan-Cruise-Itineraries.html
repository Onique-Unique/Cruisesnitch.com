How can you make the most of your time on shore excursions during an Alaskan cruise?

  • Set Your Priorities: Decide what you and your party would like to see and do, then create a plan to make the most of your time.
  • Utilize Tour Guides: Book a tour or hire a guide to give you inside knowledge of the area and allow you to explore the hidden gems.
  • Research in Advance: Research each destination ahead of time to identify what places you would like to visit and build those ideas into your plan.
  • Bring Appropriate Clothing: Temperature and weather can change rapidly in the Alaskan landscape, so make sure to bring multiple layered clothing to be prepared for any situation.
  • Pack Snacks: Bring snacks to save time and money.
  • Go on a Wildlife Tour: Take a wildlife tour to spot bald eagles and other breathtaking wildlife, such as seals, whales, and bears.
  • Visit Local Museums: Local museums, such as the Alaska State Museum, are great places to learn more about the rich, local history and culture.
  • Shop Local Stores & Markets: Shopping is a great way to get a souvenir and to support local businesses.
  • Have Fun Outdoor Activities: Go for a hike for amazing views or kayak in the pristine waters for a unique experience.
  • Have a Relaxing Day: Take a day to relax and do nothing but explore the area. Take your time and enjoy the scenery!
