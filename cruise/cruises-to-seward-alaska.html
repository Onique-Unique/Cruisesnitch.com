What are the must-see sights when cruising to Seward Alaska in 2023?

  • Kenai Fjords National Park: Get close to majestic glaciers, calving icebergs, and secluded alcoves while exploring the pristine scenery of Kenai Fjords National Park.
  • Exit Glacier: Go back in time as you walk through the 2-mile long glacier.
  • Mt. Alyeska Aerial Tramway: Enjoy stunning panoramic views of Portage Glacier and Turnagain Arm from the summit of Alyeska Mountain.
  • Alaska SeaLife Center: Explore a unique aquarium center with educational exhibits, exhibits featuring animals in their natural habitats, and interactive displays.
  • Seward Waterfront Park: Revel the stunning views of mountains and glaciers from the shoreline of this oceanfront park.
  • Iditarod National Historic Trail: Trek through this historical trail surrounded by majestic Alaskan wilderness.
  • Resurrection Bay Cruises: Take a memorable tour of the coastal area of Resurrection Bay, passing by some of Alaska's most beautiful coasts.
  • Wildwood Park: Experience the tranquility of the Alaskan wilderness while taking a stroll in this lush park.



What are some tips for packing and what essentials should you bring on your cruise to Seward Alaska in 2023?

  • Pack plenty of layers: Seward Alaska can have extreme temperatures, so make sure you have plenty of layers, such as sweaters, jackets, and scarves, to be prepared for any weather.
  • Bring a waterproof bag: Cruises usually involve plenty of activities on the water and in the rain, so make sure you have a waterproof bag to store all of your items in case of inclement weather.
  • Don't forget the sunscreen: It may snow, but the sun can be just as strong. Make sure to bring sunscreen to protect yourself from sunburn.
  • Pack binoculars: Alaska has some of the most breathtaking scenery, so make sure to bring binoculars to get a closer look.
  • Bring a camera: With Alaska's wildlife, scenery, and activities, you won't want to miss a photo op. Pack a camera to capture all of your unforgettable moments.
  • Bring comfortable shoes: You'll be doing plenty of activities on your cruise, so make sure you bring shoes that will keep your feet warm and comfortable!
  • Bring a refillable water bottle: Staying hydrated is essential while cruising. Pack a refillable water bottle to stay hydrated while sightseeing.
  • Bring a reusable bag: Plastic waste is a huge environmental issue, so make sure to bring a reusable bag for shopping and souvenir-gathering.
  • Download maps & guides: Make sure you save maps, port guides, and other Trip Information with you onto your device or a printed version to help you get around the area.
