What are the best times of year to cruise the Panama Canal and avoid crowds?

The best times of the year to cruise the Panama Canal and avoid crowds are usually in the off-season months from May to November.

Since the route is very popular in the winter months, it is best to book a cruise during the summer to enjoy an uncrowded and less expensive journey.

The water temperatures remain warm and pleasant, the rates are lower, and the number of visitors diminishes giving you the opportunity to really take in the beauty of the landscape, wildlife and culture in a relaxed, stress-free way.


Be sure to take a rain jacket and plenty of sunscreen for protection, but otherwise you are sure to love your tropical canal cruise and the chance to explore this truly unique destination.
