What are some tips for families to stay organized and make the most of their time on a family-friendly cruise?

  • Make a detailed plan: Prior to the trip, create a shared family calendar with all vacation activities clearly laid out.
  • Set reminders: Utilize a shared reminders app so everyone can be on the same page with all upcoming events during the cruise.
  • Pack light: Decide as a family what items you will need. This will prevent over-packing and make it easier to move from one activity to another in the limited space of the ship.
  • Take advantage of early dining: Book your meals early on the ship. This will allow you to have more flexibility with your time for leisure activities.
  • Map out ports: Before the trip, take time to map out the port of calls so you can get the most out of the available excursions.
  • Establish quiet times: Set aside some dedicated moment to unwind and relax as a family.
  • Divide and conquer: Everyone can get involved and take part in planning activities while performing certain tasks such as researching and booking popular attractions.
  • Keep kids entertained: Ask the cruise ship beforehand what activities they have to entertain kids of all ages.
  • Have a backup plan: No matter how detailed your itinerary is, always be prepared for unexpected delays and plan accordingly.
  • Disconnect: Take the opportunity to step away from electronics and make time for each other as a family.
