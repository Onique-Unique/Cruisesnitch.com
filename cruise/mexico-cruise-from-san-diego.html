Gobble up the deliciousness of Mexico with these seven must-try dishes on your San Diego to Mexico Cruise in 2023!

  • Tacos al Pastor: A traditional street food that packs layers of flavor, this taco is made of pork marinated with achiote paste and then cooked crispy on the trompo.
  • Tamales: Another classic Mexican favorite! These bite-sized morsels can be filled with savory ingredients such as chicken, beef, and cheese, or simply sweet with a generous helping of fruit or sweetened cream.
  • Fajitas: Another honor to the mighty taco, this dish is a combination of sautÃ©ed onions, peppers, and perfectly marinated meat, all folded into a warm flour tortilla.
  • Pozole: This traditional soup combines pork in an intense and deeply flavored broth made with guajillo chilies and hominy corn.
  • Chilaquiles: A Mexican breakfast staple made of eggs, meat, and tortilla chips layered in a chili-tomato sauce.
  • Caldo de Res: This flavorful Mexican stew is made of vegetables, beef shanks, and chilies for a deliciously healthy meal.
  • Churros: Skip the sweetener and opt for these deep-fried dough sticks topped with cinnamon and a hefty drizzle of Mexican hot chocolate sauce!
