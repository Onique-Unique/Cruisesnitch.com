The Best Cruise Ports for History Buffs in 2023

For history buffs, there's no better way to explore the world than on a cruise. With countless destinations to choose from, it can be challenging to decide which ports offer the most fascinating historical experiences.

If you're planning a cruise in 2023 and are interested in exploring the world's rich past, this guide is for you. We've curated a list of the best cruise ports for history buffs in 2023, highlighting the most intriguing sites and experiences in each destination.

  • Piraeus, Greece: Once the bustling port of ancient Athens, this destination offers a wealth of historical sites, from the Acropolis to the Keramikos archaeological site.
  • Dubrovnik, Croatia: This picturesque walled city was once an important trading port of the Venetian empire. Visitors can explore sites ranging from the grand palace and cathedral to the nearby Lovrijenac Fortress.
  • Istanbul, Turkey: Marvel at the domes and minarets of the Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia and the Topkapi Palace, a stunning reminder of this city's Byzantine and Ottoman past.
  • Cartagena, Colombia: A major port of Spain in the 16th century, Cartagena is home to some of the best-preserved colonial architecture in the Western Hemisphere.
  • La Palma, Canary Islands: Visit La Palma's historic La Alcogida area, where you'll find a variety of terraced houses and winding streets, along with the former palace of the Spanish Governor of the island.
  • Cozumel, Mexico: See the Mayan ruins at Tulum or take a tour of the island's stunning beaches, lush rainforest and ancient sites.
  • Acapulco, Mexico: Located in the same region as the ancient Aztec city of Tenochtitlan, Acapulco is full of historical sites, such as the Templo de San Diego, the Fort of San Diego, and the Museum de Arqueologia.
  • Ponta Delgada, Azores: A former trading centre of its own right, this port city is steeped in history. Explore sites like the 16th-century Igreja Matriz and the extinct volcano, Furna do Enxofre.
  • St Lucia: Visit the historical sites of Pigeon Island, the site of a major naval battle in 1778, and Castries, the island's capital city.
  • Barbados: Explore the colonial plantation houses, cathedrals and the highest point of the island, the 3,000-foot-high Mount Hillaby.

Seattle, Washington: This historic port city is home to the iconic Space Needle and is located near many of the attractions of the Puget Sound area.
