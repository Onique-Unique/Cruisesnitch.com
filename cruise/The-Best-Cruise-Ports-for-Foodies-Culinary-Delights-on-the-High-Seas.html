How can you make the most of onboard dining options and specialty restaurants during your cruise?

  • Opt for specialty restaurants: Specialty restaurants are a great way to experience a wide range of cuisines, prepared by experienced chefs.
  • Choose appetizers: Appetizers are usually a lighter and cheaper option, enabling you to try a greater range of dishes during your cruise.
  • Explore the buffet: Buffets can be a great way to sample a wider range of food, offering more choices than the normal dining room menus.
  • Enjoy a drink: Enjoy a drink with dinner, as many cruise ships offer a selection of alcoholic beverages to choose from.
  • Dine at the pool: Have a burger and beer at the poolside bar for lunch or even dinner.
  • Take advantage of events: Many cruises offer special events, such as wine tastings, signature dinners and cooking classes that provide unique dining experiences.
  • Try something new: Take the opportunity to try something new or to explore the cuisine of the ports you visit.
  • Choose an early or late sitting: Late sittings can be a great option and typically offer more relaxed dining experiences.
  • Bring treats from home: Consider bringing treats from home, like your favorite snacks, to enjoy during the cruise.
