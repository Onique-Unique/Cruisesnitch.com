What are the best strategies for finding the most affordable cruise deals in 2023?

  • Set a budget: Decide how much you are willing to pay per person on a cruise before starting your search. This'll help you narrow down your options and ensure that you don't overspend.
  • Shop around: Contact a few different cruise lines for their discounts and promotions, and compare prices to find the best deals.
  • Book early: Generally, the earlier you book a cruise, the more affordable it tends to be, so consider booking your cruise as far in advance as possible.
  • Check family or loyalty benefits: See if any family or loyalty benefits you may have can be applied to your travel to secure a good deal.
  • Utilize cruise search engines: Online cruise search engines are fantastic resources for discovering affordable cruise deals. Use them to search for the right cruise for you at the best price.
  • Consider repositioning cruises: Repositioning cruises can be an excellent option for those looking for a great deal. They are usually discounted as the cruise line needs to reposition the ship from one port to another.
  • Join cruise-specific newsletters: Sign up for cruise-specific newsletters or email lists to keep up to date on promotions as they come up. Some cruise lines may even offer exclusive discounts to newsletter subscribers.
  • Consider buy-one-get-one deals: Keep an eye out for "buy-one-get-one" deals, where you can purchase one cruise ticket and receive a second one for free.
  • Explore last-minute deals: With cruise lines needing to fill their remaining berths, they may offer steep discounts during the last minute. If you don't mind the uncertainty, this can be a great way to score some amazing deals.



How can you save money on onboard expenses such as dining, drinks, and activities?

  • Bring your own beverages on board: Yes you've read that right - Many cruise ships allow passengers to bring a limited amount of beer or wine with them especially when out at port. This will save you money on drinks throughout the cruise.
  • Purchase all-inclusive packages: Most cruise lines offer discounted packages that include meals and activities. Make sure to compare packages and find the one that fits your needs and budget.
  • Look for discounts and specials: Cruise lines often offer promotional discounts and deals on onboard activities, meals and drinks, so make sure you search for any savings that may be available.
  • Take advantage of the on-board credit: Many cruise lines offer on-board credit for excursions, meals, and drinks. Make sure to take advantage of this to save money on the expenses.
  • Purchase a beverage card: If you plan to drink often and need to stay within a budget, consider a beverage package which allows you to pay for drinks upfront at a discounted rate.
  • Stick to free activities: Most cruises offer a wide variety of free activities, such as bingo, yoga, karaoke, trivia, and more. Take advantage of these free activities to have a great time while spending less.



What are some ways to make the most of port stops while minimizing costs?

  • Go exploring the city on foot or by bike: This will not only give you the chance to explore your surroundings, but also save on costs.
  • Take part in free activities: such as walking tours or museum visits. Most port cities have a variety of free or low-cost activities.
  • Take advantage of nearby attractions: such as parks or beaches. These can all be enjoyed without spending much money.
  • Pick up locally-sourced food and supplies: This is usually much cheaper than buying on board, and offers the opportunity to experience local flavors.
  • Visit local farmers markets and bazaars: for unique and reasonably priced items.
  • Travel around by using public transportation: This is a great way to get to know an area without having to splurge on taxis or car rentals.
  • Opt for budget-friendly restaurants: As tempting as it may be to dine in pricier restaurants, there are always cheaper alternatives.
