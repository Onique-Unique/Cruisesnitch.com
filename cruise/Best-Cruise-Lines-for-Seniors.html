  • The Caribbean: Rich in culture and history, the Caribbean has something to offer everyone, no matter their age. Whether you want to go snorkeling in the turquoise waters, explore historic ruins, experience the vibrant nightlife, or simply relax on the white sandy beaches, the Caribbean has it all!
  • Mediterranean Sea: Boasting beautiful coastline and charming villages, the Mediterranean is the perfect place for those looking for a taste of the Europe and a glimpse into past times. There are plenty of activities for both active seniors and those looking to simply enjoy the sun and sample the local cuisine.
  • Alaska: A rejuvenating experience awaits seniors in Alaska. From majestic glaciers to wildlife encounters, you can boat and explore the breathtaking land. Unforgettable experiences await, such as dog sledding, and exclusive access to secret hot springs.
  • South Pacific Islands: Many of the South Pacific islands are untouched and offer seniors a chance to soak up the culture. The untouched and largely undiscovered nature of the islands attract those looking for a deeper connection with the local cultures and true adventure.

What special accommodations do cruise lines offer for seniors with mobility or health concerns?

  • Lowered beds and/or adjustable beds
  • Wider bathtubs and shower seats
  • Wheelchair-accessible cabins
  • Accessible swimming pools with wheelchair ramps or transfer chairs
  • Handicap-accessible dining areas
  • Grab bars in restrooms and hallways
  • Assisted movement room service and special meals
  • Complimentary canes and wheelchairs
  • A medical center with staff physicians
  • An on-call medical service for more complicated health concerns
