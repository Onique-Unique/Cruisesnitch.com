How to plan and budget for a family cruise vacation in 2023?

  • Set a budget: Start by setting a budget and decide how much money you want to allocate for your vacation. Consider looking at airfare, cruise packages, and excursions.
  • Research cruise dates: Check the cruise line websites for their 2023 timeline to determine the date for the best family vacation.
  • Calculate Expenses: Calculate the cost of airfare, lodging, food and other expenses to determine what is included in the cruise packages and what costs extra.
  • Identify Excursions: Research excursion packages available in the area so that your family can have additional fun outside of the cruise.
  • Create a Payment Plan: If Possible - set up a payment plan that can be used to make payments over time in order to make your vacation more affordable.
  • Set Savings Goals: Estimate how much money you need to set aside each month to reach your budget goal.
  • Make Reservations: Make travel arrangements and reservations at least four to six months before your planned departure.
  • Look for Discounts: Look for deals and discounts online and through cruise line websites that can potentially reduce the price of your cruise or excursions.
  • Finalize Documentations: Make sure you have final documents with all the necessary information for your trip.



What are the best destinations to consider when choosing a family-friendly cruise line?

  • The Caribbean: With crystal clear waters and sandy beaches, the Caribbean is the perfect destination for a family-friendly cruise! There are dozens of islands to explore, with plenty of activities to keep you busy.
  • Hawaii: offers something for everyone in the family, from upscale beachfront resorts to family-friendly activities like snorkeling and high-adrenaline sports like parasailing.
  • Alaska: Get a taste of wilderness and adventure dining one of the many Alaska cruises! Along with amazing glaciers, wildlife and a truly unique experience, Alaska offers a wide range of activities, such as heli-skiing and sea-kayaking, to keep everyone in the family entertained.
  • Mediterranean: Explore the culture, food, and architecture of the Mediterranean on a family-friendly cruise! There is so much to see and so many places to explore, meaning there are activities to suit everyone's interests!
  • Australia and New Zealand: From sparkling oceans and natural wonders, to cosmopolitan cities, Australia and New Zealand have something for everyone in the family! There are plenty of interesting shore excursion, activities and local entertainment while on this cruise.
