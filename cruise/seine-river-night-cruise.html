What are some insider tips for making the most out of my Seine River Cruise experience in Paris?

  • Make sure: you take full advantage of the audio-guided tours Listen to the experienced tour guides as they tell stories and share history about the area and its sights.
  • Dress for the weather: It can get quite windy and chilly on the river so be sure to dress in layers and bring a raincoat in case of unexpected rain.
  • Get there early: Places to stand and sit can be limited, so get there before the tour starts for the best view.
  • Take pictures: Seine River is one of the most beautiful rivers in the world and one of the most romantic places in Paris. Capture the moment on camera for an everlasting memory.
  • Taste the local cuisine: Take your cruise experience up a notch by trying some fresh, local food near the Seine River.
  • Watch the sunset: A Seine River Cruise is a great place to admire the sun setting over the river. A romantic experience that you won't forget anytime soon.
  • Mingle with locals: Don't be afraid to start up conversations with other visitors or tour guides onboard the boat. Networking is a great way to learn more about the area.
