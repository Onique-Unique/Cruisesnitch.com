How can you make the most of shore excursions during a European river cruise?

  • Research before departure: It's always best to research shore excursions ahead of your trip so you can find out which tours are available and their cost. This way, you can tailor the excursions to your interests and budget.
  • Try something new: Don't just stick to the basics - expand your horizons and explore European life by taking the time to try a new activity or explore a little-known village.
  • Take a tour guide: If a guided tour is an option for your excursion, it's often worth investing in. Not only will you get to visit the usual landmarks, but also learn about their history from a local expert.
  • Bring along a camera: As the saying goes, 'a picture paints a thousand words'; make sure you capture some great memories from your trip by bringing along a camera or phone to take photos.
  • Take the time to explore: You don't have to rush your excursions. Take time to savour the atmosphere and visit interesting stores or cafes you find along the way.
  • Soak in your surroundings: Take the time to take in views, sounds and smells of the places you visit - they're all part of your European river cruise experience.
  • Participate in events: Local markets and folk festivals, whatever is going on during your cruise, try to take the time to enjoy it and join in - this will add to your unique experience of the destination.
  • Buy souvenirs: Stocking up on local specialities and trinkets not only gives you a memento of your trip but also helps local businesses, who will appreciate the custom.
  • Network: Squeeze in a bit of networking by meeting locals or other travellers on board the ship or during a port call.
  • Have fun: Finally, if you can afford it, why not indulge a bit and treat yourself to something special? That could be anything from high tea in a castle or a spa treatment during your port call. Enjoy!



What should you know about onboard dining and entertainment options on a European river cruise?

  • Available Cuisines: Most European river cruises offer cuisine from all over the world, ranging from traditional European dishes to vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options.
  • Onboard Entertainment: Many European river cruise lines offer a variety of onboard entertainment activities, including onboard lectures, cooking classes, wine tastings, and nightly music performances.
  • Open Bars: Most European river cruise lines also offer open bars where passengers can enjoy cocktails and light snacks throughout the day.
  • Excursions: Most river cruises offer shore excursions that allow passengers to experience the best of the local culture and attractions.
  • Special Menus: Passengers can often request special menus to suit their dietary needs or preferences.
  • All Inclusive: Most cruises offer all-inclusive packages that include meals, drinks, and onboard entertainment activities.
