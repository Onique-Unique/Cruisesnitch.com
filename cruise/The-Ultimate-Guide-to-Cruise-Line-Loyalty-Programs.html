The Ultimate Guide to Cruise Line Loyalty Programs in 2023

If you're a frequent cruiser, you're likely familiar with cruise line loyalty programs. These programs offer a range of perks and benefits to repeat customers, from priority boarding to complimentary drinks and dining.

But with so many different loyalty programs available, it can be challenging to know which one to choose and how to make the most of it. That's why we've created the ultimate guide to cruise line loyalty programs in 2023. Whether you're new to cruising or a seasoned pro, this guide will help you navigate the world of loyalty programs and get the most out of your next cruise.

What are some insider tips and tricks for maximizing the benefits of a cruise line loyalty program?

  • Sign up for: direct mailers and emails from the cruise line, which often include special offers.
  • Look for: cruise line companion discounts, as these can apply to both past and new passengers.
  • Take advantage of: onboard credit, free Wi-Fi, or drink packages offered in loyalty programs, which can lower your cruise costs or add additional amenities to enhance your trip.
  • Invest in: a higher-tier loyalty program, as this can lead to even greater discounts on future cruises.
  • Participate in: special loyalty program cruise offers like Friends and Family promotions or early booking discounts.
  • Cash in: reward points that can give you upgrades, onboard credits, or discounts on future cruises.
  • Take advantage of: port discounts, pre-paid gratuities, and exclusive discounts on other services available to loyalty program members.
  • Refer friends and family: to the cruise line loyalty program, as you can both reap rewards.
  • Maximize: the benefits of a cruise line loyalty program by taking more than one cruise in a given year.
  • Reach out to customer service: if you have any questions or need assistance with using your rewards.



How do loyalty programs differ between cruise lines, and what factors should you consider when choosing a program to join?

  • Cruise Lines: Many cruise lines offer loyalty programs, including Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Norwegian and MSC Cruises. Each cruise line offers unique benefits, so it is important to consider which loyalty program is best for you.
  • Points: Loyalty programs commonly reward guests with points that can be used towards onboard purchases, special promotions and discounts. Each cruise line may offer different numbers of points per cruise, so make sure to research which loyalty program offers the most rewards.
  • Free Cruises: Some cruise lines offer members chance to earn free cruises or other rewards after reaching a certain number of points. You should check the information on the loyalty programs or call customer service to learn more about how many points are required for a free cruise.
  • Timeframes: Before signing up with a cruise line's loyalty program, you should also consider the timeframe for earning rewards. Some cruise lines offer limited-time promotions with extra points, so you may want to make sure that the loyalty program you are joining offers these types of rewards.
  • Extras: Many cruise lines also offer exclusive deals and extras for loyalty members. These could include discounts on drinks, free meals, spa services, cabin upgrades and onboard packages. Look at the details of each program to take advantage of the perks that best fit your needs.
  • Sign Up: Once you have done your research, sign-up for the cruise line loyalty program that is best for you. Make sure to provide your loyalty program number when booking future cruises so that you can maximize your rewards and enjoy the benefits each program has to offer.
