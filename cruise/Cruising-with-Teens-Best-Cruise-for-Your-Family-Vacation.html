How can you choose the best cabin or stateroom for your family when cruising with teens?

  • Research family-friendly cruise lines: Start your search for the perfect cabin or stateroom by looking into cruise lines that offer family-oriented itineraries and activities.
  • Determine the size of the room: How much space do you need in your cabin or stateroom to accommodate your family? Make sure your room is not too large or too small for your group.
  • Consider which floor you prefer: Most cruise ships have different decks with cabins and staterooms that have different features. Choose the deck and type of cabin or stateroom based on your family's needs.
  • Read reviews: Take some time to read reviews of cabins or staterooms from other families who have traveled with teens. This can help you get an idea of what to expect from the cabin or stateroom you are considering.
  • Choose a room near teen-friendly activities: Consider booking a cabin or stateroom that is close to places where teens can hang out, such as a youth center or deck with teen activities.
  • Look for discounts: Many cruise lines offer discounts during certain seasons, such as holidays or during peak travel times. These discounts can help you save money on the cost of your cabin or stateroom.
  • Ask questions: Don't hesitate to ask questions such as what type of amenities are included in the room, what payment options are available and if there is a cancellation policy. Knowing the answers to these questions can help you make an informed decision.
