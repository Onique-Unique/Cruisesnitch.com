Ahoy there, cruise enthusiast! Is the Mediterranean Sea calling your name?

You're in luck, because in 2023, you can explore its ancient ruins in all their glory on a Mediterranean cruise.

Here's a list of must-see ruins you'll get to visit:

  • The ancient city of Ephesus in Turkey: home to the iconic Library of Celsus and the Temple of Artemis.
  • The Parthenon in Athens, Greece: an enduring symbol of classical architecture.
  • Pompeii in Italy: featuring structures and artifacts preserved since the city's destruction by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD.
  • The Mausoleum of Halicarnassus in Turkey: a stunningly preserved ancient tomb of a noble, King Mausolus.
  • The grand Roman Colosseum in Italy: a testament to the sophistication and sophistication of Roman engineering.
  • Delos in Greece: a mythical birthplace of Apollo and Artemis, as well as an important religious, cultural, and trading center.

How can you prepare for the cultural and historical experiences you'll encounter on a Mediterranean cruise?

One great way to prepare for your Mediterranean cruise is to spend some time researching the historical and cultural landmarks you will visit.

There are many books, websites, and travel guides that can help you learn about the different countries, cities and sights you will see on your cruise.

This way you can learn about the history and cultural context of each place, making your trip much more meaningful.

You should also familiarize yourself with local customs so that you can fit in and feel comfortable as you explore each port.

If possible, look for festivals that may be taking place during your visit since these are great ways to get a taste of everyday life in the Mediterranean.

Another great way to prepare for the cultural and historical experiences of a Mediterranean cruise is to bring language materials with you. This can include a phrase book and a translator app to help you out when communication is necessary.

Lastly, remember to bring an open mind and an openness to explore. Learning and experiencing new things is what makes travel memorable.
