The Ultimate Guide to Planning Your First Family Cruise in 2023

Planning your first family cruise in 2023 can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can also be an exciting adventure. A family cruise provides an excellent opportunity to bond and create lasting memories while exploring new destinations.

However, to ensure a successful trip, it's essential to have a plan that covers everything from choosing the right ship to packing the right items. This ultimate guide will provide you with all the information you need to plan your first family cruise in 2023.

  • Start Planning Early: Begin your planning process right away. Choose a preferred cruise line, decide what type of cruise you want to take and determine your ideal dates and costs.
  • Map Out Your Destination: Research potential ports of call, select what excursions you are interested in and review different activities offered at each port.
  • Work Out a Budget: Determine how much you plan to spend on each vacation expense, such as food, drinks, excursions, etc.
  • Consider Cruise Amenities: Check out what amenities each cruise line offers, such as specialty restaurants, spa services, youth clubs, etc.
  • Talk to Other Travellers: Speak to other families who have taken a similar cruise to get a better idea of what to expect.
  • Book a Personal Cruise Concierge: These experts can help with pre-cruise selections such as dining reservations, spa services, shore excursions, etc.
  • Begin Packing Early: Check what items are included with your cruise package and determine what items you need to bring from home.
  • Prepare for Land Excursions: Make sure to bring the proper gear and clothing for any land/sea activities you will be taking part in.
  • Stay Connected With Technology: Purchase appropriate devices and services to stay connected while aboard.
  • Familiarize Yourself With The Cruise Ship: Read the shipboards newsletter and understand the different activities available onboard.
  • Don’t Forget To Pack Documents: Have passports and cruise tickets printed and ready to bring with you.
  • Monitor Your Finances Closely: Don’t forget to track your spending while on the cruise, so you aren't surprised with extra costs when you return home.
  • Stay Informed: Attend onboard lectures and presentations and take advantage of onboard enrichment programs.
  • Have Fun: Lastly, remember to relax and enjoy your time aboard the cruise ship!




How can you choose the best cruise line for your family's needs?

  • Start by researching your options: Look at the different cruise lines, ships, and destinations they have to offer.
  • Consider your budget: and look for specials or discounts to see who offers the best value.
  • Think about: your family's size and activity choices. Some cruise lines specialize in family cruises, or offer more activities for kids, teens, and adults.
  • Research: the safety records of each cruise line. Consider the policies and procedures regarding COVID-19.
  • Read independent reviews: from other families who have been on a cruise before. This can give you insight on staff hospitality and customer service.
  • Look at: what amenities and activities are available on each of the ships. Which ones offer the most options?
  • Make sure: there are enough activities and places to explore, so everyone will have something to do.
  • Ask family and friends for recommendations: If they've taken a cruise and had a good experience, they can provide you with useful information.
  • Choose: the cruise line that you feel meets all your needs and offers the best combination of price and amenities/activities.
  • Once you've chosen a cruise line: book early and go over all the details and policies to ensure you understand what you're signing up for.



What are the top family-friendly activities and amenities available on a cruise ship?

There’s something for the whole family to enjoy on a cruise ship!

From spectacular shows and activities to special amenities designed with families in mind, there’s no shortage of fun to be had.

Here are some of the top family-friendly activities and amenities available on a cruise ship:

  • Water parks: are a great way to cool off while sailing the high seas. Many cruise ships include a variety of water slides, pools, and splash areas to help everyone beat the summer heat.
  • After a long day of exploring the ship: don't forget to grab a family movie from the ship's library. Get comfy on the couch and enjoy some quality entertainment.
  • Many ships also offer: supervised kid's clubs, where kids can hang out with friends and take part in interactive games and activities.
  • Spectacular shows: are another great entertainment option for the whole family. From acrobats and magicians to musicals and variety shows, there’s something to suit every taste.
  • If your family is up for: something a little less active, how about a game of miniature golf or laser tag? Both these activities provide a great way to have some friendly family fun.

No matter what your family enjoys doing, there’s something for everyone on a cruise ship!



What are some tips for keeping kids entertained and engaged during a family cruise?

  • Schedule shore excursions: ahead of time to ensure there’s something planned to keep the kids engaged. Shore excursions can include exploring historical sites, visiting animal sanctuaries, or beach trips.
  • Have the kids involved: in making the travel plan. Ask them for their input on stops, meals, and activities on board the ship.
  • Give your kids their own: “ship accounts” with a certain amount of pocket money to explore and enjoy the activities onboard.
  • Take advantage: of kid’s clubs on the ship, which often provide activities like scavenger hunts, swimming, and movie showings.
  • Have family game nights: and make sure to include plenty of active games like tag, finding objects on the ship, or hopscotch.
  • Utilize technology: Bring along tablets, computers, and gaming systems if the cruise ship provides Wi-Fi.
  • When all else fails: Re-energize yourself - take a nap! Sometimes a little rest can be just the thing to keep everyone smiling and energized.
