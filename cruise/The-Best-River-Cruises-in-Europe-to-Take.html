What are the top destinations to visit on a river cruise in Europe and what makes them special?

  • Amsterdam, Netherlands: Amsterdam is the perfect destination to explore Europe on a river cruise! Nestled on the banks of the River Amstel, Amsterdam offers lots of interesting sights, such as the Anne Frank's House, the Royal Palace, and the Amstelkring Museum of Religious Art.
  • Vienna, Austria: Vienna is an enchanting city that is bursting with old-world charm. Beautifully centered on the River Danube, Vienna is filled with sights such as the iconic Schoenbrunn Palace, the Museum of Modern Art, and the Vienna State Opera.
  • Budapest, Hungary: Budapest is one of the most amazing cities on the River Danube! With tons of attractions, Budapest offers beautifully preserved medieval architecture and monuments, countless art galleries, and the Houses of Parliament.
  • Bratislava, Slovakia: Bratislava is the true cultural capital of Central Europe! On the banks of the River Danube, Bratislava boasts plenty of Baroque buildings and cobblestone streets, unfussy taverns and wonderful outdoor cafes.
  • Munich, Germany: On a river cruise, a must-see destination is Munich! This energetic city of Bavaria is an absolute treat to explore as it is situated on the banks of the River Isar and is filled with cultural attractions, such as the Olympic Park, the historic churches, and the beer halls.
  • Krakow, Poland: On the banks of the Vistula river lies one of the most beautiful cities in Europe, Krakow. With its enchanting medieval buildings and its stunning main square, Krakow offers a range of attractions such as the historical castles, the incredible art galleries, and stunning natural scenery.
