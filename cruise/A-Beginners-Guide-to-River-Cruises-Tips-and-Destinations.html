A Beginner's Guide to River Cruises in 2023: Tips and Destinations

Embarking on a river cruise in 2023 offers a unique way to explore some of the world's most stunning waterways. For beginners, the idea of a river cruise may seem daunting, but with the right tips and destinations, it can be an unforgettable experience.

This beginner's guide to river cruises in 2023 will provide you with all the information you need to plan your perfect journey, from choosing the right destination to packing the essentials.

How to select the best river cruise itinerary for your interests and preferences?

  • Consider the location: Look into which rivers the cruise visits, what nearby attractions there are, and what the climate is like during your travel dates.
  • Compare itineraries: Check out the various stops on each route and how many shore excursions they offer. Make sure they visit locations that you’d like to explore.
  • Research the vessel: Research what type of ship you’d like to sail in, from amoeba-shaped vessels to five-star luxury liners.
  • Read the reviews: Read reviews of past cruisers’ experiences with the lines and trips that you’re considering.
  • Look at the services: Review what services are offered, such as meals, entertainment, spa treatments, excursion activities, etc.
  • Know what elements: are most important for your trip - Whether it’s access to nearby attractions, offers of cultural and educational experiences, or a low-key and relaxing experience, hone in on what matters the most to you and your travel companions.
  • Inquire about discounts and promotions: Contact the cruise line and ask what specials they’re offering on the itinerary you want to book.
  • Make sure your dates are secure: Ensure that the cruise line is offering your ideal itinerary on the dates and locations that you want.



What are the must-see destinations and landmarks on a river cruise?

  • The Danube River: This magnificent river winds through Austria, Germany, Hungary, and Slovakia. A river cruise along the Danube provides breathtaking scenery, along with visits to iconic destinations and landmarks. Highlights include the historic cities of Bratislava, Budapest, and Vienna, as well as stops in quaint villages, vineyards, and castles.
  • The Elbe River: Cross from Hamburg to Dresden, with stopovers in medieval towns along the way. Take a visit to Wittenberg, Luther’s town of Reformation, iconic Berlin’s Museum Island, and beautiful Meissen, the birthplace of European porcelain.
  • The Rhine River: Experience a romantic river cruise along the Rhine, with ports of call including Amsterdam, Basel, Frankfurt, and Strasbourg. Visit fairytale castles and sip local wine as you drift past lush vineyards.
  • The Seine River: Take a leisurely voyage through breathtaking Paris, with its iconic Eiffel Tower and amazing Notre-Dame Cathedral. Cruise through charming villages and stop over in the romantic city of Rouen, with its beautiful old architecture.
  • The Douro River: Experience the timeless beauty of northern Portugal with a Douro River cruise. Stop over in Porto, the second-largest city in Portugal, and admire its stunning architecture. Meander through the dramatic mountain terrain and the stunning vineyards that stretch along the banks of the Douro River.

A river cruise is the perfect way to explore the beauty and history of Europe.

With must-see destinations and landmarks scattered across amazing rivers, you’re sure to have a memorable journey.



What are some of the unique activities and experiences available on a river cruise, and how can you make the most of them?

Looking to break away from the traditional cruise experience? River cruising is the perfect way to do just that!

With riverside ports and multiple days spent in each destination, there are plenty of unique activities and experiences to be found onboard and off.

Here are just a few ways you can put your own spin on a river cruise:

  • Get to know the locals: River cruising often takes you away from the popular tourist spots and deep into local communities. Use the extra time that a river cruise offers to get to know locals, ask about their culture, and learn about the area’s history.
  • Explore off the beaten path: Since you’ll be sailing along rivers, it’s the perfect opportunity to explore the countryside. Many cruises offer guided land tours of cities and remote villages, giving you a chance to uncover the hidden wonders of each destination.
  • Bring your own project: River cruises are the ideal time to focus on those creative projects that you’ve been wanting to complete. From brushing up on a language to writing a memoir, take advantage of the ample onboard lectures, classes, and activities on offer that can inspire you to get creative.
  • Participate in onboard events: Most river cruises feature events such as cheese tastings, cooking demonstrations, and live music. Take the time to join in on these activities and make the most of your onboard experience.

No matter how you decide to enjoy a river cruise, you’re sure to find unique experiences that you’ll remember for a lifetime.

All you have to do is take the plunge!
