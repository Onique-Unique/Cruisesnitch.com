Luxury on a Budget: Finding the Best Prices for Your 2023 Cruise

Cruising in luxury can seem out of reach for many, but with the right approach, it's possible to experience the best of cruising without breaking the bank. Finding the best prices for your 2023 cruise requires a bit of research, planning, and creativity.

From early bird discounts to loyalty programs and budget-friendly destinations, there are numerous ways to make your dream cruise a reality without sacrificing quality.

How can you identify the best time to book a cruise and save money?

  • Start planning your cruise early: Booking your trip in advance can give you access to the best deals and discounts, so start the planning process anywhere from six months to one year in advance.
  • Sign up for cruise line newsletters: Cruise lines regularly send out special offers and discounts on their newsletter subscription lists.
  • Shop around: Compare rates online and check with different travel agencies and cruise lines to see who is offering the best deals.
  • Look for last-minute deals: Cruise lines tend to offer deals to fill their vessels at the last minute. Waiting until the last minute can save you hundreds of dollars.
  • Book during shoulder season: Cruises during shoulder season (fall, late spring, early winter or late summer) tend to be cheaper than cruises during peak season.
  • Utilize the power of bidding websites: Use websites such as VacationstoGo.com or CruiseCompete.com, who offer cruise auctions and deals from several different cruise lines.
  • Consider package deals: Keep an eye out for discount packages that include flight, hotel, and cruise. These package deals can sometimes be more affordable than buying everything individually.
  • Book during certain holiday seasons: Booking cruises during major holidays such as New Yearâ€™s, Fourth of July, Thanksgiving and Christmas can often result in better rates.
  • Wander from the itinerary: If the exact date or exact type of cruise itinerary is not essential to you, you may be able to snag a lower price.
  • Choose a cabin wisely: The cabin you choose can significantly affect your rate and overall experience. Carefully weigh the amenities you desire in a cabin against what is actually included in your price.



What are some tips for choosing the right cabin category to fit your budget and preferences?

  • Determine a budget: for your cruise cabin. Consider not just the cost of the cabin, but potential associated costs such as taxes, gratuities, and specific amenities.
  • Compare prices: between different cabin categories to ensure you are getting the best value for your money.
  • Consider location of the cabin: Depending on the ship, cabins located closer to the center of the ship tend to experience less movement than those located on either side of the ship.
  • Assess cabin size and features: Compare the number of people who can inhabit the cabin, the available amenities, and square footage of the cabin.
  • Examine the balcony space options: Cabins with balconies may be slightly more expensive, but offer more room and a better view.
  • Consider the benefits: of being higher up on the ship. Higher cabins provide more breathtaking views, but may create more motion sickness.
  • Be aware: of noise levels from outside sources such as restaurants, bars, libraries, or theaters.
  • Research existing reviews: on the cabin and its location to get a better idea of your experience.
  • Utilize online resources: such as Cruise Critic to get detailed descriptions of the cabin and its attributes.
  • Only purchase: a cabin if you are comfortable with the price, amenities, and ambiance.



What are some of the best budget-friendly shore excursions and activities to enjoy during a 2023 cruise?

  • Evening cruises: Book a night cruise to take in views of the ocean at sunset.
  • Beach lounging: Search for a secluded beach to relax on without breaking the bank.
  • Fishing excursions: Take a boat out for a group fishing trip.
  • Hiking tours: Embark on a nature walk and explore nearby trails.
  • Kayaking: Rent a kayak, life vest, and paddle to enjoy the natural beauty of the area.
  • Water sports: Rent some gear and try out your favorite watersport.
  • Snorkeling/Scuba diving: Get up close and personal with the local marine life.
  • Bar or club hopping: Catch a few drinks and dance the night away.
  • Eating local: Sample the best local cuisine without breaking the bank.
