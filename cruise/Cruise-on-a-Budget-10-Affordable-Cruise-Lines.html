What are the best ways to save money when booking a cruise?

  • Book early: the earlier you book your cruise, the more money you can save. Prices can increase significantly the closer you get to your departure date.
  • Look for discount Codes and Coupons: Check for discount codes and coupons before you book your cruise. Often, certain websites or travel companies will offer discounts if you know where (and how) to look.
  • Consider Outside Sources: Consider booking outside of traditional cruise agencies, such as a group tour companies. Often, they can offer very competitive rates and can help you save money.
  • Shop around: Don't just book the first cruise you see. Take the time to compare prices of different cruises, packages, and departure dates to ensure you're getting the best possible price.
  • Travel During Off Peak Seasons: Peak times, such as the summer and holidays, are usually more expensive. Try to book your cruise during the off-peak times or shoulder season to get cheaper rates.
  • Book Multiple Cruises: If you plan on taking more than one cruise, book them all at once for a discount. Cruise lines will often offer discounts for those who book more than one cruise at the same time.
