What are the 10 most in-demand positions on a cruise ship and why?

  • Culinary Work: From cooks to dishwashers, the cruise ship needs a lot of people to prepare, serve and clean up after meals, which makes this one of the most in-demand positions on a cruise ship.
  • Housekeeping: Keep the ship immaculate and help the guests stay comfortable during their voyage.
  • Waitstaff/Bar Tenders: Provide drinks and food to passengers, help clean up after meals and generally help keep the cruise shipâ€™s environment a pleasant one.
  • Cruise Director: Responsible for creating and maintaining an enjoyable environment for guests on board, organizing activities and entertainment, and taking care of passenger needs throughout their cruise.
  • Maintenance/Engineering: Responsible for keeping the ship running smoothly while at sea and docked, they keep life-saving equipment in top condition and also perform repairs.
  • Security/Safety Officer:play a critical role in the safety and security of the ship by monitoring security and safety equipment, inspecting cabins and common areas, and providing safety training for guests.
  • Hospitality Staff: Work long hours, assist with guest needs and maintain the overall hospitality of the cruise ship.
  • Retail Staff: Stock and maintain ship shops and provide assistance with purchases for guests.
  • Spa Employees: Train guests, help them with relaxation and treatments, and keep facilities clean and orderly.
  • Casino Workers: Run games, provide security, and ensure guests have an enjoyable casino experience during their voyage.



Are there any additional benefits or perks that come with working on a cruise ship beyond salary?

  • Travel: You have the opportunity to travel the world - and get paid to do it!
  • Accommodation: You'll be provided with comfortable on-board accommodation while travelling.
  • Variety of work: You'll gain varied work experience and skills while working in different departments and roles onboard.
  • Discounts: You'll usually receive a discount on any merchandise and services, such as spa treatments and souvenirs, on board the ship.
  • Food: You'll have access to delicious, good meals and snacks frequently if not all day, every day.
  • Learning: You'll develop a range of skills and gain knowledge about different cultures and languages during your on-board experience.
  • Networking: You'll be able to meet and connect with a variety of people from around the world.
  • A sense of community: It's an incredible way to make lifelong friends and create a lasting network.
