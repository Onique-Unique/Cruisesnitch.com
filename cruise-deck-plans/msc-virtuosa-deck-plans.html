Favorite highlights of the MSC Virtuosa

The ship is well-designed and easy to navigate, and there's something for everyone on board. Here's a more detailed look at some of the key features:

  • Cabins: The MSC Virtuosa offers a wide variety of cabin types, from inside cabins to balcony suites. All of the cabins are comfortable and well-appointed, and they feature modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and mini-fridges.
  • Dining: The MSC Virtuosa has 14 restaurants and food bars, so you're sure to find something to your taste. There are several main dining rooms, as well as specialty restaurants serving everything from Italian cuisine to sushi.
  • Entertainment: The MSC Virtuosa has a wide range of entertainment options, including a casino, theater, and multiple bars and lounges. There's also a kids' club and a spa, so you can relax and rejuvenate while on board.



What to know for making the most of your MSC Virtuosa cruise

  • Book your cabin early: The MSC Virtuosa is a popular ship, so cabins can book up quickly. If you have a specific cabin type in mind, it's best to book early to ensure that you get the one you want.
  • Choose a dining package: The MSC Virtuosa offers a variety of dining packages, so you can choose the one that best suits your budget and needs. If you're a foodie, you might want to consider one of the specialty dining packages.
  • Make reservations for entertainment: The MSC Virtuosa's theater and casino are very popular, so it's a good idea to make reservations in advance. You can do this online or through the MSC Cruises app.
  • Take advantage of the ship's technology: The MSC Virtuosa has a lot of great tech features, so be sure to take advantage of them. You can use the interactive screens to book shore excursions, check the weather, and even order food and drinks.
  • Have fun!: The MSC Virtuosa is a great ship to have fun on. There's something for everyone on board, so be sure to relax and enjoy your vacation.
  • Specialty Restaurants: The MSC Virtuosa offers a variety of specialty restaurants, including the Crown Grill, the Salty Dog Gastropub, and Sabatini's Italian Trattoria.

If you're looking for a cruise ship that has it all, the MSC Virtuosa is a great option. With its comfortable cabins, delicious food, and plenty of entertainment, you're sure to have a memorable vacation.
