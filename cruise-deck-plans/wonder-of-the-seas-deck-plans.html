  • First of all, the Wonder of the Seas has the tallest slide at sea. The Ultimate Abyss is a 10-story slide that will take your breath away.
  • Second, the Wonder of the Seas has the first floating sports bar at sea. The Perfect Storm Pool Bar is a great place to watch the game while enjoying a refreshing drink.
  • Third, the Wonder of the Seas has the first dedicated space for kids and teens on a Royal Caribbean ship. The Adventure Ocean is a fun and safe place for kids to play and learn.
  • And finally, the Wonder of the Seas has the first glow-in-the-dark laser tag arena at sea. The Clash for the Crown is a great activity for the whole family.

So, if you're looking for a cruise ship that has everything, the Wonder of the Seas is the ship for you. With its amazing amenities and unique features, there's something for everyone on this ship.



Favorite highlights of the Wonder Of The Seas

So, let's start at the top:

  • On deck 17 and 18: you'll find the Suite Neighborhood. This is where all the fancy suites are, and it's also where you'll find the exclusive Solarium pool. The Solarium is a great place to relax and escape the crowds, and it's also got a retractable roof, so you can enjoy it even if the weather isn't perfect.
  • On deck 16: you'll find the Ultimate Abyss, which is the tallest slide at sea. You'll also find the Perfect Storm water park, which has three slides and a splash pad. And if that's not enough water for you, there are also four swimming pools on deck 16.
  • On decks 15 and 14: you'll find the Royal Promenade. This is the heart of the ship, and it's where you'll find most of the shops, restaurants, and bars. There's also a casino and a nightclub on deck 15.
  • On decks 13, 12 and 11: you'll find the staterooms. There are a variety of stateroom types to choose from, including inside cabins, ocean view cabins, balcony cabins, and suites.
  • On decks 10 and 9: you'll find the Central Park neighborhood. This is a beautiful area with real trees and plants. It's a great place to relax and take a break from the hustle and bustle of the ship.
  • On decks 8 and 7: you'll find the Boardwalk neighborhood. This is a fun and energetic area with shops, restaurants, and rides. There's also a carousel and a roller coaster.
  • On decks 6 and 5: you'll find the Vitality at Sea Spa & Fitness Center. This is a great place to relax and pamper yourself. There's also a bowling alley and a skating rink on deck 6.
  • And on decks 4 and 3: you'll find the dining rooms and the theater. There are a variety of dining options to choose from, including a buffet, a la carte restaurants, and specialty restaurants.



Tips for making the most of your Wonder Of The Seas cruise

  • Book your excursions early: The most popular excursions tend to sell out quickly.
  • Take advantage of the ship's many amenities: There's something for everyone on this ship, so don't be afraid to try new things.
  • Make sure to see the shows: The Wonder of the Seas has some of the best entertainment at sea.
  • Don't forget to relax and enjoy yourself: You're on vacation, after all!
  • Try the Adventure Ocean kids' club: It's a great place for kids to play and learn while you enjoy some adult time.
  • Make sure to wear: plenty of sunscreen, hats, and sunglasses. The sun can be strong at sea.
  • Be prepared for crowds: Cruise ships can be busy, especially during the peak season.
