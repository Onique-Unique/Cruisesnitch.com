The Sky Princess is one of the largest and newest cruise ships in the Princess Cruises fleet. It was launched in 2019 and refurbished in 2023. With a capacity of over 3600 passengers, the Sky Princess offers a variety of amenities and activities for all ages.



Favorite highlights of the Sky Princess

The Sky Princess has a wide variety of public spaces to enjoy, including:

  • Piazza: The Piazza is the heart of the ship and is home to a variety of shops, restaurants, and bars.
  • Princess Theater: The Princess Theater is a state-of-the-art theater that hosts a variety of shows and performances.
  • Crown Grill: The Crown Grill is a specialty restaurant that offers classic American cuisine.
  • Salty Dog Gastropub: The Salty Dog Gastropub is a casual restaurant that offers a variety of pub fare.
  • Horizon Court Buffet: The Horizon Court Buffet is a casual buffet restaurant that offers a variety of international cuisine.



What to know for making the most of your Sky Princess cruise

  • Inside Cabin: Inside cabins are the most affordable type of cabin and do not have a window.
  • Oceanview Cabin: Oceanview cabins have a window with a view of the ocean.
  • Balcony Cabin: Balcony cabins have a private balcony with a view of the ocean.
  • Suite: Suites are the largest and most luxurious type of cabin and offer a variety of amenities, such as a private balcony, a living room, and a separate bedroom.
  • Main Dining Room: The Main Dining Room is a formal restaurant that offers a variety of international cuisine.
  • Specialty Restaurants: The Sky Princess offers a variety of specialty restaurants, including the Crown Grill, the Salty Dog Gastropub, and Sabatini's Italian Trattoria.
  • Casual Restaurants: The Sky Princess also offers a variety of casual restaurants, such as the Horizon Court Buffet, the Slice Pizzeria, and the Swirls Ice Cream Parlor.
